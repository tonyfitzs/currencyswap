App Name: Convertr
Tagline: Know What It Costs.

Short Description:
A fast, location-aware currency conversion app that shows you what prices really mean — in your home currency.

Full Description:
Convertr is the smart way to travel and shop abroad. Just open the app, type in a local price, and instantly see what it costs in your home currency — no dropdowns, no clutter.

It automatically detects your location and sets the appropriate conversion. No accounts, no ads, no tracking — just instant clarity.

Features:
• Automatic location-based currency detection
• One-time home country setup
• Clean, simple interface
• Works offline with stored rates
• Optional manual override

Your privacy matters. We don’t collect or transmit any personal data. Ever.
